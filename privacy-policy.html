Privacy Policy

How CrystalFlow Miami collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: March 13, 2026

CrystalFlow Miami ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website crystalflowmiami.com and provides water treatment installation services in Miami-Dade County, Florida. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, schedule an appointment, make a purchase, or otherwise interact with our services.

01

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you interact with our services, including:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number, and home address, collected when you book an appointment through Calendly or contact us directly
  • Service address: the physical address where water treatment services will be performed
  • Communication records: messages and interactions through our Intercom customer support chat
  • Marketing preferences: email address, name, and communication preferences collected through Klaviyo email subscription forms
  • SMS consent: phone number and messaging consent provided through Twilio-powered communications

Payment Information

When you make a purchase, payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. We do not store, process, or have access to your full credit card number, debit card number, or bank account details. Stripe collects and securely processes your payment information in accordance with PCI DSS standards. We receive only a confirmation of your transaction and the last four digits of your card for reference purposes.

Usage Data and Cookies

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website, including:

  • Device information: browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and device identifiers
  • Browsing behavior: pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns, and referring URLs
  • Network information: IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Analytics data: collected through Google Analytics 4 (GA4) using cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Advertising data: collected through Google Ads for conversion tracking and remarketing purposes
  • Email engagement: open rates, click-through rates, and browsing behavior tracked by Klaviyo

Social Media Data

If you interact with us through Instagram, certain profile information and engagement data may be collected through our Klaviyo integration for marketing purposes.

02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: to schedule appointments, perform water testing, install and maintain water treatment systems, and process payments
  • Communications: to respond to your inquiries, send appointment confirmations and reminders, and provide customer support through Intercom
  • Marketing: to send promotional emails and SMS messages about our services, special offers, and water quality information through Klaviyo and Twilio (only with your consent)
  • Analytics and improvement: to understand how visitors use our website, improve our services, and optimize user experience using Google Analytics 4
  • Advertising: to deliver relevant ads and measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns through Google Ads remarketing
  • Legal compliance: to comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
03

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share your information only with the following service providers who assist us in operating our business:

  • Stripe: payment processing and transaction management
  • Klaviyo: email marketing, SMS marketing, and customer engagement
  • Calendly: appointment scheduling and booking management
  • Google Analytics 4: website traffic analysis and user behavior insights
  • Google Ads: advertising, conversion tracking, and remarketing
  • Twilio: SMS communications and messaging services
  • Intercom: customer support chat and communication
  • Netlify: website hosting and content delivery
  • Render: API hosting and backend services

Each of these service providers is contractually obligated to protect your information and may only use it to perform services on our behalf. We may also disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

04

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect usage data and improve your experience. The following tracking technologies are active on our site:

Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

We use GA4 to analyze website traffic, understand user behavior, and improve our services. GA4 uses first-party cookies to distinguish unique users and sessions. Data collected includes pages visited, session duration, traffic sources, and demographic information. Google may use this data in accordance with its own privacy policy.

Google Ads Remarketing

We use Google Ads cookies to track conversions (such as booking a water test appointment) and to show you relevant ads on Google's Display Network and search results after you visit our website. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Klaviyo Tracking

Klaviyo places cookies on our website to track browsing behavior, identify returning visitors, and personalize email marketing content. This helps us send you more relevant communications based on your interests and interactions with our website.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or alert you when a cookie is being set. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features on our website.

05

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: you may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: you may request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Deletion: you may request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions
  • Opt-out of marketing: you may unsubscribe from our marketing emails at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email, or by contacting us directly. To opt out of SMS messages, reply STOP to any message
  • Opt-out of tracking: you may opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Data portability: you may request your personal data in a structured, commonly used format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@crystalflowmiami.com or call (786) 947-3824. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

06

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted through our website
  • PCI DSS-compliant payment processing through Stripe
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure hosting through Netlify and Render with industry-standard security practices
  • Regular review of our data collection, storage, and processing practices

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data to the best of our ability.

07

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Specifically:

  • Customer records: retained for the duration of our business relationship and for a reasonable period afterward to support warranty claims, maintenance records, and potential future service needs
  • Payment records: retained as required by applicable tax and accounting regulations
  • Marketing data: retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Website analytics data: retained according to Google Analytics 4 default retention settings (14 months for user-level data)
  • Customer support records: retained in Intercom for a reasonable period to improve service quality and resolve any outstanding issues

When personal information is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

08

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information as promptly as possible. If you believe that we may have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at info@crystalflowmiami.com.

09

California Residents — CCPA Rights

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) provide you with additional rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to know: you have the right to request that we disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the categories of sources from which it was collected, the business purpose for collecting it, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it
  • Right to delete: you have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions
  • Right to correct: you have the right to request that we correct inaccurate personal information
  • Right to opt out of sale or sharing: we do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising
  • Right to non-discrimination: we will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA/CPRA rights

To submit a request, contact us at info@crystalflowmiami.com or call (786) 947-3824. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request. You may also designate an authorized agent to make a request on your behalf.

10

Florida Residents

As a business operating in Florida, we comply with applicable Florida privacy laws. While Florida does not currently have a comprehensive consumer privacy statute equivalent to the CCPA, we are committed to transparent data practices for all of our customers in Miami-Dade County and throughout the state.

Florida's Security of Communications Act protects against unauthorized interception of electronic communications. We do not intercept or monitor your private communications. Any recordings of customer service calls will be disclosed at the time of the call.

Florida's Information Protection Act requires us to notify affected individuals in the event of a data breach involving personal information. In the unlikely event of a security breach affecting your data, we will notify you in accordance with Florida law and take immediate steps to mitigate any potential harm.

If you are a Florida resident and have questions about how your data is handled, please contact us at info@crystalflowmiami.com.

When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you via email or a prominent notice on our website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
