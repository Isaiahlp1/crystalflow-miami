Frequently Asked Questions

Miami's tap water meets EPA standards, but meeting minimum standards and being truly clean are different things. Our testing of Miami homes regularly finds elevated chlorine, limestone hardness, trace PFAS compounds, and other contaminants that affect taste, health, and appliances. A free water test reveals exactly what's in your water.

Under-sink reverse osmosis installations are typically completed in 2–3 hours. Whole-home water softener or RO system installations take 4–6 hours depending on your home's plumbing configuration. We handle everything cleanly — our technicians leave your home as they found it.

Pricing depends on your specific water quality results, home size, and which system is the right fit. We don't quote prices before testing — because an undersized or oversized system is a waste of your money. Your free water test gives us everything we need to recommend the most cost-effective solution for your exact situation.

Our certified technician visits your home with professional-grade testing equipment to measure hardness, total dissolved solids (TDS), pH, chlorine, chloramines, iron, and other local contaminants. The entire visit takes about 45 minutes and ends with a clear written report and a personalized recommendation — no obligation to purchase anything.
